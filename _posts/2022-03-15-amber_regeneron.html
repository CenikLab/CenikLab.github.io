Amber was awarded $150,000 by winning the third place at the National Regeneron Science Talent Search among 1800 participants . She received this wonderful award for her project in the lab developing a novel algorithm to detect ribosome pause sites using Bayesian wavelet thresholding techniques. Previously, she won the top oral and written presentation awards at the 38th annual Research Science Institute the top oral and written presentation awards at the 38th annual Research Science Institute.
