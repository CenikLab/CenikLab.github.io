The Center for Excellence in Education (CEE) awards Amber Luo the top oral and written presentation awards at the 38th annual Research Science Institute. During her summer research, Amber developed a novel algorithm to detect ribosome pause sites using Bayesian wavelet thresholding techniques. We congratulate Amber for her remarkable success!
